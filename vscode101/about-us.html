About

Us

Welcome, internet travellers, to our informational science website. We are governed by a group of high school students with a passion and desire to share science, engineering, and technology. If you’re here for research or for school, or just have some free time, you’re welcome to stop by and learn something new!
Our articles will be a mix of informative and question-answering, and should be a moderate reading level. If you have any questions, submit them here and we just might feature it. Each week we’ll be posting new, fresh science content to satisfy your curiosities, so stay tuned!
